1. Objective of Learning

English: The objective is to shatter common myths about electricity. We will learn that electricity is NOT invisible empty energy, nor is it the flow of neutrons. We will discover the ultimate truth: Electrons move very slowly, but the Electromagnetic ENERGY FIELD moves at the speed of light! We will prove this using AC, DC, and Wireless Charging.

नेपाली: यसको उद्देश्य विद्युत्‌को बारेमा रहेका सामान्य भ्रमहरू तोड्नु हो। हामी सिक्नेछौं कि विद्युत् कुनै अदृश्य खाली ऊर्जा होइन, न त यो न्यूट्रोनको प्रवाह हो। हामी अन्तिम सत्य पत्ता लगाउनेछौं: इलेक्ट्रोनहरू धेरै बिस्तारै हिँड्छन्, तर विद्युत चुम्बकीय ऊर्जा क्षेत्र (Electromagnetic Energy Field) प्रकाशको गतिमा अघि बढ्छ! हामी AC, DC, र वायरलेस चार्जिङको प्रयोग गरेर यो प्रमाणित गर्नेछौं।

Sim 1: What is electricity? (Inside a Copper Wire)

A wire is made of atoms. The heavy nucleus is locked in place. The tiny Electrons are free to jump from atom to atom.

Wait, if they barely move, why do we show them moving? Because their slow, physical "Drift Velocity" is required to create the current and the magnetic fields! No movement = no electricity. (We just sped up the animation by 10,000x so you can see them drift!)

Sim 2: Generator to House (The "Marble Tube" Effect)

If electrons move very slowly (millimeters per second), how does the light turn on instantly when the power plant turns on?

The Marble Analogy: The wire is already full of electrons. When the generator pushes ONE electron in, it pushes its neighbor, causing a chain reaction. The Energy Wave travels at the speed of light, even though the actual Electrons barely moved!

Sim 3: Alternating Current (AC) vs Direct Current (DC)

DC (Batteries): Electrons flow continuously in one single direction like a river.

AC (Wall Outlets): Generators pull and push electrons back and forth 50 times a second (50Hz). They vibrate in place, but still transfer energy perfectly!

Sim 4: Wireless Charging (Proof of Electromagnetic Energy)

If energy was *only* carried by moving electrons, wireless charging would be impossible!

The Truth: The AC current in the charger creates an expanding and collapsing Magnetic Field. This invisible field flies across the empty air gap at the speed of light and pushes the electrons inside your phone's battery. The Field is what actually moves the energy from place to place!

3. Main Vocabulary & Meanings

Electron (\(e^-\))

English: A tiny, negatively charged particle inside an atom. The physical movement of these particles is electricity. Neutrons and Protons do not flow in wires!

नेपाली: परमाणु भित्र रहेको एक सानो, ऋणात्मक चार्ज भएको कण। यी कणहरूको भौतिक चाल नै विद्युत् हो। न्यूट्रोन र प्रोटोनहरू तारमा बग्दैनन्!

Drift Velocity (\(v_d\))

English: The incredibly slow speed at which an electron actually moves down a wire (often less than a millimeter per second).

नेपाली: इलेक्ट्रोन वास्तवमा तारमा हिँड्ने अविश्वसनीय रूपमा सुस्त गति (प्रायः प्रति सेकेन्ड एक मिलिमिटर भन्दा कम)।

Direct Current (DC)

English: Electric current that flows continuously in one single direction. Created by batteries and solar panels.

नेपाली: एउटै दिशामा निरन्तर बग्ने विद्युत् प्रवाह (दिष्ट धार)। यो ब्याट्री र सौर्य प्यानलहरूबाट सिर्जना गरिन्छ।

Alternating Current (AC)

English: Electric current that rapidly reverses its direction back and forth. Created by massive generators and used in our home outlets.

नेपाली: विद्युत् प्रवाह जसले आफ्नो दिशा छिटोछिटो अगाडि र पछाडि उल्टो बनाउँछ (प्रत्यावर्ती धार)। विशाल जेनेरेटरहरूद्वारा सिर्जना गरिएको र हाम्रो घरको प्लगहरूमा प्रयोग हुन्छ।

Magnetic Field (B)

English: The invisible energy field created by moving electrons. This field is what actually carries the electrical energy through space at the speed of light.

नेपाली: चलिरहेका इलेक्ट्रोनहरूद्वारा सिर्जना गरिएको अदृश्य ऊर्जा क्षेत्र (चुम्बकीय क्षेत्र)। यो क्षेत्रले नै वास्तवमा विद्युतीय ऊर्जालाई प्रकाशको गतिमा अन्तरिक्ष हुँदै बोक्छ।

Electromagnetic Induction

English: The process where a changing magnetic field creates a voltage in a nearby wire, allowing wireless energy transfer (Faraday's Law).

नेपाली: परिवर्तनशील चुम्बकीय क्षेत्रले नजिकैको तारमा भोल्टेज सिर्जना गर्ने प्रक्रिया, जसले ताररहित ऊर्जा स्थानान्तरणलाई सम्भव बनाउँछ (फ्याराडेको नियम)।

4. Conclusion

English: In conclusion, electricity is a combination of slow matter and lightning-fast energy fields. The Electrons (never neutrons) physically move, but they only drift slowly. This slow movement creates an invisible Electromagnetic Field that rushes around the wires at the speed of light. As proven by wireless charging, the actual energy pushing your devices isn't the physical particle crossing a gap, but the invisible magnetic waves carrying the power!

नेपाली: निष्कर्षमा, विद्युत् भनेको सुस्त पदार्थ र बिजुली जत्तिकै छिटो ऊर्जा क्षेत्रहरूको संयोजन हो। इलेक्ट्रोनहरू (न्यूट्रोन कहिल्यै होइन) भौतिक रूपमा हिँड्छन्, तर तिनीहरू बिस्तारै मात्र सर्छन्। यो सुस्त चालले एउटा अदृश्य विद्युत चुम्बकीय क्षेत्र (Electromagnetic Field) सिर्जना गर्छ जुन प्रकाशको गतिमा तारहरूको वरिपरि कुद्छ। वायरलेस चार्जिङले प्रमाणित गरेजस्तै, तपाईंको उपकरण चलाउने वास्तविक ऊर्जा कुनै खाली ठाउँ पार गर्ने भौतिक कण होइन, तर शक्ति बोक्ने अदृश्य चुम्बकीय छालहरू हुन्!
